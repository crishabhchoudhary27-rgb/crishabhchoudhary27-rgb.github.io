Research Project

ADHD Support Chatbot with Explainable AI (XAI)


Developing an NLP-based conversational AI system for ADHD support using Transformer models (BERT/GPT-2), combined with Explainable AI techniques (SHAP, LIME, Attention Rollout) to mathematically interpret model decisions. The system aims to bridge the gap between black-box AI and clinical interpretability in mental health applications.
